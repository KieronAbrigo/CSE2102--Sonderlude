Welcome to The Guyana Prison Service!

 
The primary responsibility of the Prison Service in Guyana is vested in the Minister of Public Security. The Guyana Prison Service operates as an arm of the Criminal Justice System. It is headed by a Director, assisted by other essential staff inclusive of a Deputy Director, two (2) Senior Superintendent, two (2) Superintendent, a Superintendent responsible for the training of ranks and the retraining of inmates and an Assistant Superintendent responsible of the Finance Sector.
